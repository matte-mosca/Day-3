Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions. tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become vour new favorite holidav starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1 3/4 (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1 1/4 extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan
  • 1 1/4 extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrave rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dought

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dought ingridients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
